01

Reliable Token Farming

Snacks, the system’s bonding tokens, aren’t affected by external
forces, meaning that the system can never lower the rate. This is
thanks to an internal bonding curve.

Moreover, Btc/Eth snacks are linked directly to the
intermediate asset, Zoinks, to hedge the risk of volatility.

As an added bonus, due to holders staking rewards, the
holder’s losses are minimised and their profits are
maximised.

02

Simplicity of Use

As a result of Zoink’s liquid pair with BUSD, the asset may be
accessed through popular crypto exchanges. No additional
knowledge or time is required from investors.

Moreover, pre-made smart contracts such as the LunchBox and
PULSE are automated to free up the time of the investor.

The system is designed to be used by traditional investors,
combatting fears of extreme volatility in the crypto markets.

03

Profitability for the User

Traders are able to provide liquidity through Snacks, multiplying
their investment while earning on improvements in the
currency’s rate.

Zoinks can be traded for BUSD on the site at a 1:1 ratio, but sold
for more accross various decentralised exchanges.
